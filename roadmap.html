The road ahead - Post Workshop Update!

During the final discussion, we tried to work out a roadmap for the proposal of a ADHO Special Interest Group (SIG) in Digital Literary Stylistics

  • elect a contact person (intermediary with ADHO Steering Committee): Berenike Herrmann
  • work out a description of the SIG (mission, anticipated activities and rationale, and list of its membership) - see minutes
  • set up work space and resources (such as web site, zotero db, tools) - visit the wiki sace
  • decide on future meetings (at DH conferences, other, possible funding)
